# KartvyaCSR - Project Summary

## What is KartvyaCSR?

**KartvyaCSR** is a full-stack web platform that connects **Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs)** with **Corporations** to facilitate Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) partnerships. Think of it as a "LinkedIn for CSR" - a digital marketplace where companies can find verified NGOs to partner with for their social impact initiatives, and NGOs can showcase their projects and receive funding.

## The Problem It Solves

In India, companies are legally required to spend a portion of their profits on CSR activities (Companies Act, 2013). However, finding trustworthy NGOs, managing partnerships, tracking fund utilization, and ensuring compliance can be challenging. KartvyaCSR simplifies this entire process by providing:

- **Transparency**: Verified NGOs with document verification
- **Efficiency**: Easy discovery and partnership management
- **Compliance**: Tracking and reporting for legal requirements
- **Accountability**: Fund utilization tracking with detailed breakdowns

## Key Features

### For NGOs:
- 📝 **Profile Creation**: Register and create a detailed profile showcasing their mission, focus areas, and past work
- 💼 **Project Management**: List and manage their social impact projects
- 🤝 **Partnership Requests**: Receive and manage partnership requests from corporations
- 💰 **Fund Tracking**: Track how CSR funds are being utilized with detailed breakdowns
- 📊 **Analytics Dashboard**: View partnership statistics, funding trends, and project progress
- 💬 **Communication**: In-app messaging system to communicate with corporate partners
- 📄 **Document Verification**: Automated verification of registration certificates and compliance documents

### For Corporations:
- 🔍 **NGO Directory**: Browse and search a directory of verified NGOs with filters (focus area, verification status, location)
- ⭐ **Shortlisting**: Save interesting NGOs to a shortlist for later review
- 🤝 **Partnership Creation**: Create and manage partnerships with NGOs
- 💰 **CSR Fund Management**: Allocate, commit, and track CSR fund disbursements
- 📈 **Compliance Tracking**: Monitor partnership progress and generate compliance reports
- 📊 **Analytics Dashboard**: View funding statistics, partnership metrics, and impact reports
- 💬 **Communication**: Direct messaging with NGO partners
- 📤 **Data Export**: Export reports in CSV format for record-keeping

## Technology Stack

### Frontend:
- **React 19** - Modern UI framework
- **Vite** - Fast build tool
- **Tailwind CSS & Bootstrap** - Beautiful, responsive design
- **Recharts** - Data visualization and analytics
- **React Router** - Navigation

### Backend:
- **Node.js & Express** - Server framework
- **PostgreSQL** - Database for storing all data
- **JWT** - Secure authentication
- **Puppeteer** - Automated document verification
- **Multer** - File upload handling

## How It Works

1. **Registration**: NGOs and Corporations register separately with their credentials
2. **Profile Setup**: Users create detailed profiles (NGOs add projects, Corporates add company info)
3. **Discovery**: Corporations browse the NGO directory, filter by criteria, and shortlist potential partners
4. **Partnership**: Corporations create partnership requests, NGOs accept/decline
5. **Funding**: Once partnered, corporations allocate CSR funds to specific projects
6. **Tracking**: NGOs report fund utilization with detailed breakdowns and photos
7. **Analytics**: Both parties can view dashboards with statistics, charts, and reports
8. **Communication**: Built-in messaging system for seamless communication

## Real-World Impact

This platform helps:
- **Corporations** fulfill their CSR obligations efficiently and transparently
- **NGOs** access funding and build sustainable partnerships
- **Society** benefit from better-organized social impact initiatives
- **Regulators** ensure compliance through transparent tracking
